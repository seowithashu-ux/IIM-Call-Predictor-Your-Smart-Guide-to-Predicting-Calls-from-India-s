## What Is an IIM Call Predictor?

An **IIM Call Predictor** is an intelligent analysis tool designed to estimate your chances of receiving interview calls from different IIMs.  
It compares your profile with:

- Previous years’ cut-offs  
- Official IIM shortlisting policies  
- Category-based criteria  
- Profile weightages  

This provides a highly accurate expectation of possible calls even before official results are released.

You don’t have to wait for months—get a quick evaluation of your chances right away.

### 2. Sets Realistic Expectations  
If the chances for top-tier IIMs are low, you can target excellent alternatives like **MDI, SPJIMR, IIFT, FMS**, or **IIT B-Schools**.

### 3. Boosts Your WAT–PI Preparation  
Knowing likely calls early allows you to prepare essays, SOPs, and interviews for specific IIMs.

## How an IIM Call Predictor Works

A reliable predictor evaluates your profile using four major components:

### 1. CAT Score & Percentile  
The biggest factor—higher percentiles significantly improve your chances.

### 2. Academic Profile  
Scores in 10th, 12th, and graduation matter, as many IIMs value consistent performance.

### 3. Work Experience  
Relevant work experience can earn extra weightage in several IIMs, especially for PGP programs.

### 4. Category  
Cut-offs for GEN, OBC, SC, ST, and EWS categories differ, and predictors adjust results accordingly.
